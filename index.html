Kabila Yetu Ni Kenya

Our Tribe is Kenya

Kabila Yetu Ni Kenya is a unity-driven movement built on patriotism, responsibility, and national identity. We choose peace and purpose over division, empowering our citizens socially, economically, and politically for a stronger, united Kenya.

Our Vision

A country where unity guides decisions, young people lead with values, and every Kenyan contributes to shaping a peaceful and progressive nation.

Pillars of the Movement

Unity Above Tribe

We embrace our national identity and promote peace, dignity, and togetherness.

Social Responsibility

We encourage active citizenship, community engagement, and supportive leadership.

Accountability

We uphold transparency, integrity, and participation in public life.

Economic Empowerment

We support skills, innovation, and opportunities for youth empowerment.

Movement Oath

“I choose Kenya above tribe. I commit to unity, peace, and responsibility. I stand for truth, justice, and the well-being of every citizen. I am a builder of my nation. Kabila Yetu Ni Kenya.”
