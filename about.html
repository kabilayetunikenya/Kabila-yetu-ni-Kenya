About Kabila Yetu Ni Kenya

Kabila Yetu Ni Kenya is a civic and social movement rooted in the belief that our national identity must rise above tribe, division, and fear — and be anchored in shared responsibility, dignity, and unity.

Who We Are

Kabila Yetu Ni Kenya is a citizen-driven movement bringing together Kenyans from all backgrounds to promote national cohesion, civic awareness, and responsible participation in social, political, and economic life.

We are not a political party. We are a unifying platform that encourages dialogue, accountability, and active citizenship — especially among young people and communities at the grassroots.

Why We Exist

Kenya’s greatest strength lies in its diversity — yet division has often weakened our collective progress. We exist to shift the narrative from tribe to nation, from apathy to responsibility, and from silence to informed engagement.

Where We Are Going

Our vision is a united Kenya where citizens are informed, engaged, and empowered to shape the future of their country beyond election cycles and political seasons.

National Unity

Building a culture of cohesion and respect that transcends ethnic, social, and political lines.

Civic Awareness

Empowering citizens with knowledge, dialogue, and platforms to engage meaningfully in national conversations.

Community Action

Supporting initiatives that uplift communities, protect the vulnerable, and nurture future generations.

Patriotism with Purpose

Encouraging love for country expressed through responsibility, integrity, and service.
